# Content Guidelines

**Last Updated:** November 13, 2025
**Version:** 1.0

## Purpose

These Content Guidelines govern all content uploaded, displayed, or distributed through the Red Square platform. Our goal is to create a safe, inclusive, and professional advertising environment for all users.

## Overview

As a user of Red Square, whether you're an advertiser uploading content or a screen owner displaying it, you must ensure that all content complies with these guidelines, our Terms of Service, and all applicable laws.

---

## 1. Prohibited Content

The following types of content are **strictly prohibited** on Red Square:

### 1.1 Illegal Content
❌ Content that violates any local, state, national, or international law
❌ Content promoting illegal activities
❌ Counterfeit goods or services
❌ Stolen intellectual property
❌ Pyramid schemes or illegal multi-level marketing

### 1.2 Adult and Sexual Content
❌ Pornography or sexually explicit content
❌ Nudity or partial nudity (with limited exceptions for art/education)
❌ Sexual services or escort services
❌ Sex toys or adult products
❌ Dating services with sexual emphasis

### 1.3 Violent or Disturbing Content
❌ Graphic violence or gore
❌ Content depicting or promoting self-harm
❌ Content depicting animal cruelty
❌ Shocking or disturbing imagery
❌ Content glorifying or promoting violence

### 1.4 Hate Speech and Discrimination
❌ Content that promotes hatred or discrimination based on:
  - Race or ethnicity
  - Religion or beliefs
  - National origin
  - Gender or gender identity
  - Sexual orientation
  - Disability
  - Age
  - Veteran status
❌ Symbols, images, or language associated with hate groups
❌ Dehumanizing language or imagery

### 1.5 Dangerous or Harmful Content
❌ Instructions for creating weapons or explosives
❌ Promotion of dangerous stunts or challenges
❌ Content promoting eating disorders
❌ Instructions for hacking or fraud
❌ Promotion of self-harm or suicide

### 1.6 Misinformation
❌ Deliberate spread of false information
❌ Manipulated media designed to deceive
❌ Health misinformation (e.g., fake cures, anti-vaccine propaganda)
❌ Election misinformation
❌ Conspiracy theories promoting harm

### 1.7 Harassment and Bullying
❌ Content targeting individuals for harassment
❌ Doxing (sharing personal information)
❌ Threats or intimidation
❌ Cyberbullying

### 1.8 Spam and Deceptive Practices
❌ Misleading or deceptive advertising
❌ Clickbait or false promises
❌ Fake reviews or testimonials
❌ Get-rich-quick schemes
❌ Content designed solely to drive traffic elsewhere

### 1.9 Intellectual Property Violations
❌ Copyrighted content used without permission
❌ Trademark infringement
❌ Counterfeit goods
❌ Plagiarized content

### 1.10 Privacy Violations
❌ Content containing others' personal information without consent
❌ Hidden cameras or unauthorized recordings
❌ Content violating confidentiality agreements

### 1.11 Malware and Security Threats
❌ Content containing viruses, malware, or spyware
❌ Phishing attempts
❌ Links to malicious websites
❌ QR codes leading to harmful content

## 2. Restricted Content (Requires Additional Review)

The following content types are permitted with restrictions and may require additional review:

### 2.1 Alcohol
✓ Allowed with restrictions:
  - Must comply with local alcohol advertising laws
  - Cannot target minors
  - Must include responsible drinking messaging where required
  - Must adhere to time/location restrictions

### 2.2 Gambling and Gaming
✓ Allowed with restrictions:
  - Must have proper licensing
  - Cannot target minors
  - Must include responsible gambling messaging
  - Must comply with local gambling laws

### 2.3 Financial Services
✓ Allowed with restrictions:
  - Must be from licensed institutions
  - Must include required disclosures
  - Cannot make unrealistic promises
  - Must comply with financial advertising regulations

### 2.4 Healthcare and Pharmaceuticals
✓ Allowed with restrictions:
  - Prescription drugs: Must comply with FDA/local regulations
  - Medical services: Must be from licensed providers
  - Must include required warnings and disclosures
  - Cannot make false health claims

### 2.5 Political Content
✓ Allowed with restrictions:
  - Must clearly identify who paid for the ad
  - Must comply with election laws
  - Subject to blackout periods before elections (where required)
  - Must be factually accurate

### 2.6 Weapons and Firearms
✓ Allowed with restrictions (where legal):
  - Must comply with local and federal laws
  - Cannot promote illegal weapons
  - Must include safety messaging
  - May be prohibited in certain locations

### 2.7 Cryptocurrency
✓ Allowed with restrictions:
  - Must comply with financial regulations
  - Cannot make unrealistic promises
  - Must include risk disclaimers
  - Must be from legitimate providers

## 3. Content Quality Standards

All content must meet minimum quality standards:

### 3.1 Technical Quality
✓ Minimum resolution: 1920x1080 (Full HD)
✓ Acceptable file formats: MP4, JPG, PNG, GIF
✓ Maximum file size: 50MB
✓ No corrupted or incomplete files
✓ Audio must be clear (if applicable)

### 3.2 Visual Standards
✓ Images must be clear and in focus
✓ Text must be legible from viewing distance
✓ Colors should not cause discomfort (e.g., excessive flashing)
✓ Aspect ratio must match screen specifications

### 3.3 Professional Standards
✓ Content should be professional and appropriate for public display
✓ No excessive profanity or crude language
✓ Content should be appropriate for all ages (unless in restricted area)
✓ Branding and messaging should be clear

---

## 4. Content Review Process

### 4.1 Automated Review
- All content undergoes automated scanning for:
  - Prohibited content types
  - Copyright violations
  - Malware or security threats
  - Quality standards

### 4.2 Manual Review
- Content may be flagged for manual review if:
  - Automated systems detect potential issues
  - Users report content
  - Content falls into restricted categories
  - Screen owners request review

### 4.3 Review Timeline
- Most content is reviewed within 1-24 hours
- Restricted content may take longer (up to 3 business days)
- Express review available for premium users

### 4.4 Appeals
- If content is rejected, you may appeal the decision
- Provide additional information or context
- Appeals are reviewed within 2 business days

---

## 5. Screen Owner Responsibilities

Screen owners have the right and responsibility to:

✓ Review content before it displays on their screens
✓ Reject content that doesn't align with their values or local community standards
✓ Set additional content restrictions for their screens
✓ Remove content immediately if issues arise
✓ Report problematic content to the platform

**Note:** Screen owners cannot discriminate based on protected characteristics.

---

## 6. Advertiser Responsibilities

Advertisers must:

✓ Ensure they have rights to all content uploaded
✓ Obtain necessary licenses, permissions, and model releases
✓ Comply with all applicable advertising laws
✓ Provide accurate information
✓ Respect intellectual property rights
✓ Include required disclosures and disclaimers

---

## 7. Enforcement

### 7.1 Content Removal
- We may remove content that violates these guidelines
- Content may be removed with or without notice
- Repeat violations may result in account suspension

### 7.2 Account Actions
- **Warning:** First minor violation
- **Temporary Suspension:** Repeated or moderate violations
- **Permanent Ban:** Severe or repeated violations

### 7.3 Refund Policy for Rejected Content
- Content rejected before display: Full refund
- Content removed during campaign: Pro-rated refund
- Content violating guidelines: No refund

---

## 8. Reporting Violations

If you see content that violates these guidelines:

1. Use the "Report" button on the content
2. Select the violation type
3. Provide details and evidence
4. Submit the report

We review all reports and take appropriate action.

## 9. Special Categories

### 9.1 Political Advertising
- Must include "Paid for by" disclosure
- Must comply with local election laws
- May require verification of advertiser identity
- Subject to additional restrictions during election periods

### 9.2 Advocacy and Issues
- Issue-based advocacy is permitted
- Must be factually accurate
- Cannot promote discrimination or hate
- May require additional disclosure

### 9.3 Children's Products
- Must comply with COPPA and local laws
- Cannot make false claims about safety or benefits
- Must include appropriate warnings
- Cannot collect data from children

### 9.4 Environmental Claims
- Must be substantiated
- Cannot make false "green" claims
- Must have evidence for sustainability claims

## 11. Seasonal and Time-Based Restrictions

- Near specific dates (e.g., no election ads within 48 hours of voting)
- During local events or holidays
- In school zones during school hours
